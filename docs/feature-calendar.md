# Module Guide: Calendar & Appointments

The Calendar is the scheduling hub of your clinic. It manages appointment bookings, doctor rosters, and availability slots.

---

## 1. Navigating the Calendar Grid

Click the **Calendar** tab to view your clinic's schedules. You can customize the calendar view to suit your needs:
*   **Day View:** Focus on a single day's schedule, showing hourly slots and conflict warnings.
*   **Week View:** Monitor weekly doctor rosters.
*   **Month View:** Look ahead at monthly bookings.
*   **Filters:** Filter appointments by doctor or department to check specific schedules.

## 2. Scheduling an Appointment

### How to Book an Appointment:
1. Click the desired time slot on the calendar grid.
2. Search for the patient by entering their name or phone number.
3. Select the target department and doctor.
4. Add the reason for the visit (e.g., Routine check-up, Consultation).
5. Click **Save**. The system will automatically register the appointment and send a confirmation message to the patient.

## 3. Rescheduling & Cancellations

Exelth makes it easy to handle schedule changes:
*   **Rescheduling:** Click and drag the appointment block to a new time slot on the calendar grid.
*   **Cancellations:** Click the appointment card, select **Cancel**, and choose a cancellation reason. The system will automatically notify the patient.
*   **Doctor Leave Override:** Use the emergency block function to mark a doctor as unavailable. The system will automatically flag conflicting appointments and allow you to reschedule them.
