## Accessing the Platform

### Logging In
1. Open your clinic's Exelth login URL in a web browser.
2. Enter your registered email address and password.
3. Click **Continue** to access your dashboard.

### The Sidebar Menu
Once logged in, you will see a navigation sidebar. Depending on your role in the clinic (Admin, Doctor, Receptionist, or Staff), some options may be hidden to ensure security and privacy:

*   **Home (Dashboard):** Daily schedules, setup progress checklists, and recent activities overview.
*   **Calendar:** Create, update, and manage appointments.
*   **Tasks:** View clinic assignments, checklists, and patient call lists.
*   **Inbox:** Communicate directly with patients via SMS and WhatsApp messages.
*   **Organization:** Set up branch facilities, departments, and staff details.
*   **Reception:** Track the live patient check-in queues and register walk-ins.
*   **Patients:** View the Electronic Health Records (EHR) registry, vitals histories, and past prescriptions.
*   **Labs:** Track laboratory test orders and upload diagnostic PDF files.
*   **Payments:** Process client invoices, checkout billing records, and settle payments.
*   **Workflow:** Track patient wait times and monitor automated communication rules.
*   **Integrations:** Connect automated services like WhatsApp Business templates.
*   **Analytics:** View reports on daily patient counts, clinic utilization, and lab revenues.
*   **Settings:** Customize notification templates, profile settings, and payment details.
