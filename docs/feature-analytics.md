# Module Guide: Analytics Reports & Settings

This guide details the Analytics Reports dashboard and system settings in Exelth.

---

## 1. Clinic Analytics Reports

The Analytics dashboard provides real-time reports on clinic performance, helping managers optimize operations:

*   **Patient Volume Charts:** Monitors daily, weekly, and monthly appointment counts to check clinic utilization.
*   **Operational Capacity:** Tracks patient waiting times and consultation durations to find bottlenecks.
*   **Revenue Metrics:** View total income from consultations, prescriptions, and lab orders.

## 2. System Settings

Customize your workspace under the **Settings** panel:

*   **Profile Settings:** Update your personal information, profile photo, and password.
*   **Facility Configurations:** Manage clinic names, addresses, and logo files.
*   **Members & Ranks:** Manage staff directories, assign system roles, and edit access levels.
*   **Notification Channels:** Customize template text for automated SMS and WhatsApp messages.
*   **Payment Keys:** Connect merchant accounts (e.g., Razorpay) to process online payments.
*   **System Logs:** Review security audit logs and change histories.
