# Module Guide: Patient Registry & EHR Profiles

The Patients module serves as the central directory for your clinic's patient records, histories, and medical files.

---

## 1. Searching the Patient Directory

Find and manage patient records in the directory:
*   Navigate to the **Patients** tab.
*   Search for a patient using their full name or phone number.
*   Filters allow you to view active patients, inactive patients, or new registrations.

---

## 2. Registering a Patient

Add new patient records to the system:
1. Click the **Add Patient** button.
2. Fill out the form: Name, Phone Number, Date of Birth, Gender, Address, and Emergency Contact details.
3. Save the record. The system will automatically generate a unique patient ID.

## 3. Viewing the Patient Profile (EHR)

Click a patient's name to open their profile, which contains their complete medical history organized by section:
*   **Vitals History:** Logs vital signs (blood pressure, temperature, weight, heart rate) across visits, displaying changes over time.
*   **Clinical Consultation Notes:** Detailed logs from past consultations.
*   **Prescriptions:** Active and past prescriptions. You can view, print, or download prescription statements as PDF files.
*   **Lab Reports:** View laboratory orders, sample collection dates, and download uploaded result PDFs.
*   **Bills & Invoices:** Settle unpaid invoices, view transaction history, and export payment statements.
*   **Insurance details:** Track insurance providers, policy numbers, and view uploaded files.
