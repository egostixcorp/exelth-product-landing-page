# Role Manual: Doctors & Medical Staff

This guide covers the consultation dashboard, electronic health records (EHR) entry, prescription writing, and laboratory ordering workflows managed by the clinic's medical practitioners.

---

## 1. Navigating Your Daily Consultations Queue

Keep track of checked-in patients waiting to see you:

1. Navigate to the **Reception** tab.
2. Select your name from the Doctor Filter to show only your active queue.
3. Look under the **Waiting** column. Patients are sorted by check-in time. A color-coded badge indicates their wait duration (Green < 15m, Yellow < 30m, Red > 30m).
4. Click **Start Consultation** on the next patient. This updates their status to **In Consultation** and opens the clinical notepad.

---

## 2. Writing Clinical Notes & Prescriptions

Inside the active consultation view, you can log clinical histories and prescribe medications:

### Recording Medical History
*   Log presenting symptoms, vitals (BP, heart rate, temp), and initial diagnoses.
*   Review the patient's past clinical summaries, historical prescriptions, and prior lab reports from the side drawer.

### Issuing Electronic Prescriptions (E-Rx)
1. Scroll down to the **Prescriptions** section.
2. Enter the medication name, dosage (e.g., 500mg), frequency (e.g., Once daily, After food), and duration (e.g., 5 days).
3. Once completed, click **Approve Prescription**. The system automatically compiles the E-Rx PDF and makes it available on the patient's companion B2C Care App.

## 3. Ordering Lab Tests

If the patient requires diagnostic evaluations:

1. Under the consultation view, click the **Order Lab Test** action block.
2. Search and select tests from your clinic's catalog (e.g., Complete Blood Count, Blood Glucose).
3. Add any clinical instructions for the lab technician.
4. Click **Submit Lab Order**. This adds the order to the laboratory dashboard queue.

---

## 4. Completing visits & Scheduling Follow-ups

*   **Complete Visit:** Click **Complete Consultation** to close the session, moving the patient's file to the reception checkout billing list.
*   **Book Follow-up:** If the patient requires another evaluation, click **Schedule Follow-up** to select a date and time. This logs the scheduled follow-up and closes the current consultation session.
