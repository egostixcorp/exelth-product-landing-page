# Module Guide: Organization & Staff Rosters

The Organization module manages clinic details, medical departments, and the staff directory.

---

## 1. Managing Facility Branches

Clinic managers can update facility branches:
1. Open the **Organization** tab.
2. Edit active profile metrics: Branch Name, Address, Contact Number, and default logo imagery.
3. Save changes. This updates headers on patient bills, prescriptions, and lab reports.

---

## 2. Managing Departments & Specialties

Group doctors and staff members by department (e.g., General Medicine, Pediatrics, Gynaecology, Labs):
1. Navigate to **Organization** → **Departments**.
2. Create departments and configure their operational hours.
3. Assign doctors to departments. This links them to the correct calendars and appointment options.

## 3. Staff Roster & Leave Management

*   **Roster Directory:** Manage names, contact numbers, and roles (Admin, Doctor, Receptionist, Staff) for all clinic staff.
*   **Emergency Leave Handling:** If a doctor is away unexpectedly, use the emergency block function to mark them as unavailable. The system will automatically notify the reception desk, flag conflicting appointments, and prevent new bookings for the leave dates.
*   **Access Control:** Enable or disable staff log-ins to manage security and keep the staff list current.
