# Role Manual: Front Desk & Receptionists

This guide details the daily check-in, queue scheduling, and payment collection workflows managed by the front desk staff.

---

## 1. Patient Check-In & Walk-in Registration

When a patient arrives at the clinic, your first step is to check them in or register their details.

### How to Check In a Scheduled Patient:
1. Navigate to the **Reception** tab.
2. Locate the patient's name under the **Scheduled** tab list.
3. Click the **Check In** button.
4. This moves the patient to the **Waiting (Arrived)** lobby list, notifying the assigned doctor.

### Registering Walk-in Patients:
1. Under **Reception**, click the **Register Walk-in** button.
2. Fill out the patient details: Full Name, Contact Number, Gender, and Age.
3. Select the desired medical specialty (Department) and the doctor.
4. Click **Submit** to automatically check them in and queue them in the waiting lobby.

---

## 2. Managing Appointment Bookings

Use the Calendar tab to schedule future appointments or update current bookings.

### Booking a New Appointment:
1. Open the **Calendar** tab.
2. Locate the desired date and time slot, then click on it.
3. Search for the patient by name or phone number.
4. Choose the department and doctor.
5. Save the appointment. The system will send a confirmation message to the patient automatically.

### Rescheduling or Cancelling:
*   **Reschedule:** Drag and drop the appointment card to a new time block on the calendar grid.
*   **Cancel:** Click the appointment card and select **Cancel Appointment**. Choose the reason for cancellation (e.g., patient request, doctor unavailable) and save.

---

## 3. Invoice Settlements & Checkout Payments

Once the patient completes their consultation, you will process their billing statements.

### Settle Bill:
1. Navigate to the **Payments** tab.
2. Locate the patient's active billing statement (marked as **Unpaid** or **Draft**).
3. Click **Process Payment**.
4. You can select the payment method: Cash, Card, UPI, or click **Generate Payment Link** to send a Razorpay UPI checkout link directly to the patient's phone via SMS or WhatsApp.
5. Once the transaction completes, print the invoice receipt or send it to the patient.
