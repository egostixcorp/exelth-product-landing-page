# Role Manual: Clinic Administrators & Owners

This guide covers the administrative duties and setup configurations managed by the Clinic Administrator or Owner.

---

## 1. Setup & Branch Configurations

As the administrator, you can configure the clinic profiles and shift rosters.

### Managing Facility Profiles
1. Navigate to the **Organization** tab.
2. Edit the active Facility profile to enter details such as the hospital name, physical address, and official contact phone numbers.
3. Toggle between multiple branches using the location selector.

### Setting Up Departments
1. Navigate to the **Organization** → **Departments** section.
2. Add new medical categories (e.g., General Medicine, Pediatrics, Gynaecology, Lab Services).
3. Assign registered practitioners to their respective specialties.

### Staff Directory Roster
1. Navigate to the **Organization** → **Staff** section.
2. Add new practitioners, receptionists, or lab technicians.
3. Configure their access roles (Admin, Doctor, Receptionist, Staff) to set system permissions.
4. Toggle active status to remove staff members from shift assignments when on leave.

## 2. Managing Subscription Plans & Quotas

Exelth runs on tier-based subscription packages. Administrators can track and manage usage levels from the settings portal:

*   **View Active Plans:** Navigate to **Settings** → **Billing & Plans** to check the clinic's active subscription tier (Free, Growth, or Enterprise).
*   **Track Quotas:** Review current usage limits (e.g., patient records filled, lab orders triggered, SMS messages sent).
*   **Upgrade Subscriptions:** Choose and activate premium packages to increase facility capacity limits.

---

## 3. Integrations & Communications Setup

Administrators configure the connection to third-party services:
*   **WhatsApp Connector:** Connect the clinic's WhatsApp Business number and approve templates for automated confirmations and results.
*   **Payment Keys:** Connect your Razorpay merchant ID under **Settings** → **Payments** to enable online checkout links.
