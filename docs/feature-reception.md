# Module Guide: Reception desk & Patient Queues

The Reception module manages daily patient queues, check-ins, and consultation status tracking.

---

## 1. Walk-in Registration

Register walk-in patients quickly at the front desk:

1. Navigate to the **Reception** tab.
2. Click the **Register Walk-in** button.
3. Fill out the patient details: Name, Phone Number, Gender, and Age.
4. Select the department and the doctor.
5. Click **Submit**. This checks the patient in and adds them to the waiting queue.

## 2. Managing the Live Waiting Queue

The Reception dashboard organizes patient visits by status:

- **Scheduled:** Patients booked for today who have not yet arrived.
- **Waiting (Arrived):** Checked-in patients waiting in the lobby. Their wait times are tracked and color-coded (Green < 15m, Yellow < 30m, Red > 30m) to help prioritize care.
- **In Consultation:** Patients currently in session with a doctor.
- **Completed:** Finished visits waiting for billing checkout or prescription printouts.

## 3. The Consultation Log

Doctors can access the patient record directly from the waiting queue:

1. Select the next patient in the **Waiting** queue.
2. Click **Start Consultation** to begin the visit and open the medical file.
3. Record vitals, symptoms, and diagnoses.
4. Add medications to the prescription list and order lab tests if needed.
5. Schedule a follow-up date and click **Complete Consultation** to close the session.
