# Clinic User Guide: Marketing Profile Setup

The Marketing Profile Setup is a multi-step configuration wizard that helps your clinic establish its public-facing digital profile. Once completed, this profile enables patients to find your clinic, learn about your services, view your medical team, and book appointments online.

## Detailed Step-by-Step Setup Guide

### Step 1: Basic Information
*   **Purpose:** Establish your clinic's primary identity.
*   **What to enter:**
    *   Official Clinic Name (as it should appear on invoices and reports).
    *   Short Tagline or Mission Statement.
    *   Brief Description summarizing your clinic's specialties and patient care philosophy.

### Step 2: Branding & Media
*   **Purpose:** Customize your clinic's visual identity on patient portals, receipts, and invoices.
*   **What to enter:**
    *   Clinic Logo (high-quality PNG/JPG format).
    *   Primary Brand Color (used to style digital receipts and client communications).
    *   Gallery Photos of the lobby, consultation rooms, or exterior to help patients identify your clinic.

### Step 3: Contact & Location
*   **Purpose:** Help patients locate your clinic and contact your front desk.
*   **What to enter:**
    *   Primary Support Phone Number & Official Email Address.
    *   Physical Address (Street details, City, and Postal Code).
    *   Map Coordinates or Google Maps placement pin.

### Step 4: Facilities & Equipments
*   **Purpose:** Showcase your clinic's diagnostic and therapeutic capabilities.
*   **What to enter:**
    *   Available ward options (e.g., General Wards, Private Rooms, ICU availability).
    *   Diagnostic Equipment listings (e.g., X-Ray machines, MRI scanners, Ultrasound gear).

### Step 5: Services Offered
*   **Purpose:** List the treatments, diagnostic categories, and medical specialties available at your clinic.
*   **What to enter:**
    *   Select active departments (e.g., Pediatrics, General Medicine, Cardiology).
    *   List specific procedures offered and set their base consultation fees.

### Step 6: Team & Specialties
*   **Purpose:** Introduce your doctors and medical team to build patient trust.
*   **What to enter:**
    *   List active practitioners and highlight their specialties.
    *   Add qualifications, educational background, certifications, and consultation schedules.

### Step 7: Preview & Confirmation
*   **Purpose:** Review all entered details before publishing.
*   **What to do:**
    *   Examine the layout draft from the patient's perspective.
    *   Click **Publish** to make your marketing profile live and start accepting online patient bookings.
