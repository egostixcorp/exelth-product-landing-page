# Module Guide: Tasks & Inbox Communications

This guide explains how to manage clinic tasks and handle patient communications from the Inbox.

---

## 1. Clinic Tasks

The Tasks panel is an operational checklist that keeps your staff organized.

### Core Features
*   **Daily Action Items List:** View all operational tasks assigned to you or your department, such as "Call patient for feedback," "Verify billing error," or "Call back for follow-up scheduling."
*   **Task Lifecycle:** Tasks are grouped by status (Pending, Overdue, and Completed). Mark tasks as complete by clicking their checkboxes.
*   **Automatic Tasks:** The system automatically generates tasks for specific events, such as when a lab technician uploads a high-priority lab result that requires immediate review.

## 2. Patient Inbox

The Inbox is a central messaging hub where you can communicate with patients via SMS and WhatsApp.

### Core Features
*   **Patient Chats List:** Renders a list of patient message threads on the left, with active conversations highlighted.
*   **Live Conversations:** Click a patient thread to view their message history and type responses directly in the chat panel.
*   **Automated Log:** The chat log automatically records all system-sent messages, such as booking confirmations and lab report updates, keeping the communication history in one place.
