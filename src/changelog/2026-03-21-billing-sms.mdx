date: "2026-03-21"
product: "infra-platform"
tags: ["billing", "feature"]
summary: "When you create a bill, Exelth can now send a payment link directly to the patient's phone. They can pay from anywhere, without visiting the clinic."
---

- A **payment link is sent to the patient's phone number** when a bill is created
- Patients can open the link and pay from their phone — no app or login required
- Payment status updates automatically once the patient completes the payment
