- New **Settings → Payments** section with six configuration areas: bank details, UPI, accepted payment methods, payment gateway, payout schedule, and tax settings
- All settings are saved and applied immediately across your clinic
- Conditional fields appear based on what payment methods or gateway you choose — no clutter
