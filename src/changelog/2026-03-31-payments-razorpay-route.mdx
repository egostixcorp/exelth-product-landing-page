- **Connect your clinic's bank account** directly from Exelth settings to receive settlements automatically
- Patients can now **pay their bill from a shared link** — useful for sending via SMS or WhatsApp
- Once the patient pays, the bill status in your dashboard updates automatically
- Account verification status is visible in your clinic settings
