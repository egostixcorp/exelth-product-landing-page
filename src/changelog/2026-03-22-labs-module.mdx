date: "2026-03-22"
product: "care-app"
tags: ["labs", "feature"]
summary: "The Labs module is fully live — manage your test catalog, book lab orders with a slot picker, collect payments at booking, and automatically notify patients with preparation instructions."
---

- **Lab catalog** now supports sample type, fasting requirements, home collection availability, and turnaround time for each test
- Test categories are now selected from a dropdown instead of typed manually — keeping your catalog consistent
- **Book a lab order** with a slot picker to choose the collection time before confirming
- **Quick actions** directly on the orders list — mark a sample as collected or cancel an order without opening a separate page
- Click any **summary card** to filter the orders list; date range filter also available
- Patients automatically receive **preparation instructions via SMS** when a lab order is placed
- **Daily revenue widget** added to the Labs dashboard
- **Collect payment at the time of booking** — supports cash, UPI, card, and insurance
