---

- **Click any summary card** — such as Pending, Paid, or Overdue — to instantly filter the payments list to that group
- New **status filter** to quickly find bills by their current state
- New **date range filter** to view payments within any time period
- New **payment method filter** to see only cash, UPI, card, or insurance payments
- All filters can be combined together at the same time
